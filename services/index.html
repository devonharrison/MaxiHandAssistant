The Most Common Jobs Maxwell Helps With

Loose taps, flickering lights, doors that don’t close properly, walls that need a fresh coat of paint – Maxwell takes care of the everyday repair and maintenance jobs that keep your home running smoothly.

Below are some of the most requested services, but if you don’t see your task listed, you can still reach out. If it’s around the house or office and needs fixing, installing or tidying up, there’s a good chance Maxwell can help.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of jobs can I book you for? expand_more
Maxwell handles most small to medium handyman tasks – things like minor plumbing and electrical work, shelving, cupboard and door repairs, painting, patching walls and general maintenance. If you’re unsure, just describe the job when you get in touch and you’ll get an honest answer on whether it’s a good fit.
Do you charge a call-out fee? expand_more
Yes, there is a standard call-out fee which covers travel and the first part of the inspection. From there you’ll receive a quote for the work. For bigger jobs, the call-out fee can often be included in the final price.
Do I need to supply my own materials? expand_more
You’re welcome to supply materials if you already have them, or Maxwell can source what’s needed and add it to the quote. This will be discussed upfront so there are no surprises.
Which areas do you work in? expand_more
Maxwell mainly serves Cape Town and surrounding suburbs. Service areas include the CBD, Northern Suburbs, Southern Suburbs and parts of the Atlantic Seaboard. If you’re just outside these areas, send a message with your location and we’ll see what’s possible.
