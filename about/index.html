Maxwell’s Story

Maxwell is a hardworking and dependable handyman based in Cape Town, with practical experience in property maintenance, gardening, painting, furniture repairs, and general household support.

His hands-on background includes caring for private properties, helping with day-to-day upkeep, and keeping homes clean, functional, and well maintained. His experience also includes basic plumbing, roof repairs, furniture fixing, domestic work, and outdoor maintenance.

Maxwell takes pride in honest work, good communication, and helping clients with the jobs that keep a home running smoothly. Whether it’s fixing small issues, tidying outdoor spaces, or assisting with general maintenance, he brings care and reliability to every job.
